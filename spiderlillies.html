Spider Lillies Breathe Together (2024) w. bela and Jena Jang

"Guests enter the interactive sound installation in darkness and silence. The zen-like geometry in the arrangement of the space seems to indicate what will happen and in what mood. A red lily flower as a speechless and peaceful observer at each illuminated microphone station. The guests understand the opening instructions and move freely, quietly and fluidly through the space. Sound grows non-invasively in the silent installation.

Squeaking, beeping, croaking, hooting, grunting, munching, whining, howling, moaning, scratching, knocking, squeaking, whistling. Sounds and experiences intensify.

bela, Jena and mvd0ae disappear into the crowd at times, I can only guess where they are by their grunts, growls, grunts and other guttural vocal expressions. The dynamics, rhythm, harmony, shape and colour of the sound are in a compact and holistic unity.

Nothing stands out, everything is interwoven and connected in space, in time, in movement.

It is not the grand gestures that surprise, but the intimate actions and details. The symbolism of the red spider lilies offers a journey into other worlds and, together with the ambient sound, evokes the Epic of Gilgamesh.

They evoke a sense of impermanence and eternity, touching on themes of mortality, transformation, love, separation and loss. The time comes, just when we are not expecting it yet, and the actors begin to disappear from the hall. They gently disappear in silence. The symphony continues with a contrast of swinging creaks and beats".

Words and photographs by micharu Micha for FullMoonZine.
