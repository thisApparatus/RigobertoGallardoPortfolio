Testigo en la Polvadera (2024)

Scars are the echoes of violence. Places that have served as the unwilling arenas for brutality carry the weight of attestation. The land remembers and through its presence it conveys. The graphic score for the musical piece "Testigo en la Polvadera" was produced by digitally manipulating photographs of rural areas of Bosnia & Herzegovina. The marks on the architecture seemed to scream the same screams heard in places far away but burdened with similar memories, and similar scars.

Through a of sound it demands to be received as a multifaceted experience, touching on symbol, materiality and textural focus.

Intergenerational trauma and the bond between witnesses of violence, places and individuals, goes beyond words and resonates somewhere between the sound of the rustle of the leafs, the holes in the walls and the constant flow of a river.

"Testigo en la Polvadera" is a part of the upcoming album "Ciclos de Entumecimiento".
